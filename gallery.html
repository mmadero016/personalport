Space All At Once
Space All At Once was created as a UI/UX prototype, with its concept revolving around the emphasis of space exploration and the universe. The UI was created and executed through Figma, with a focus on a vibrant and sleek interface that connects with sapce. The website was made functional using HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, and allows users to input a location, where they will see whether an aurora is visible at that location. The functional website is under construction and will be found here.

PieceIT
PieceIT is a UI/UX concept that allows users to visualize and piece together personalized rooms based on their preferences. The app is designed to help users create their ideal living space by selecting furniture, colors, and layouts that suit their style. The UI was created and executed through Figma, focusing on a user-friendly interface and a modern aesthetic.

HERO: Healthy Eating, Real Outcomes
HERO was created as a UI/UX prototype, with its concept revolving around the emphasis of health and wellness for all types of individuals, giving users the access to assess their health needs in a personalized manner – no matter their age, health conditions, or personal goals. The UI was created and executed through Figma.

MTA Ridership: 2020 & 2024
This Python project analyzed changes in New York City's metro ridership following the COVID-19 pandemic. Using a dataset spanning from 2020 to 2024, it leveraged user inputs to generate interactive maps based on variables such as borough, transit type, payment method, and more. The project was developed using JupyterHub. You can view a more detailed explanation here.

WaddlePup
WaddlePup is a concept that gives individuals a place to highlight and track the health and wellbeing of their pet dogs. The wireframe and logo were created using Figma. The website was made functional using HTML, CSS, and React.

Swissted Flyers
The flyers were created following Swissted concepts, created by Mike Joyce and reimagines concert flyers through the lens of Swiss modernist design. Using the principles of Swiss typography, featuring clean grids, bold colors, and sans-serif typefaces. This flyer was created using Figma.

Mexican Student Association at SU & SUNY ESF Logo
The Mexican Student Association at SU & SUNY ESF is a well-established student organization on campus. The logo was created shortly after I began my role as graphic designer, illustrated using Procreate and finalized with Canva.
