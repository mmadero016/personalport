PieceIT
PieceIT is a UI/UX concept that allows users to visualize and piece together personalized rooms based on personal preferences. The app is designed to help users create their ideal living space by selecting furniture, colors, and layouts that suit the user's style.

HERO
HERO was created with its concept revolving around the emphasis of health and wellness for all types of individuals. HERO gives users the access to assess their health needs in a personalized manner, no matter their age, health conditions, or personal goals.

Swissted
The flyers were created following Swissted concepts, created by Mike Joyce and reimagines concert flyers through the lens of Swiss modernist design. Using the principles of Swiss typography, featuring clean grids, bold colors, and sans-serif typefaces. This flyer was created using Figma.

Logo for the Mexican Student Association at SU & SUNY ESF
The Mexican Student Association at SU & SUNY ESF is a well-established student organization on campus. The logo was created shortly after I began my role as graphic designer, illustrated using Procreate and finalized with Canva.

Programming

Space All At Once
Space All At Once was with its concept revolving around the emphasis of space exploration. The site will allow users to input a location, where they can see whether an aurora is visible at the location inputed. The website is under construction and will be found here soon.

MTA Ridership: 2020 & 2024
This Python project analyzed changes in New York City's metro ridership following the COVID-19 pandemic. Using a dataset spanning from 2020 to 2024, it leveraged user inputs to generate interactive maps based on variables such as borough, transit type, payment method, and more.

WaddlePup
WaddlePup is a concept that gives individuals a place to highlight and track the health and wellbeing of their pet dogs. The wireframe and logo were created using Figma. The website was made functional using HTML, CSS, and React.

The site is designed to be visually appealing and easy to navigate, with a focus on showcasing my work and experience.
