Syracuse Game Day

Highlighting the start of football season, this promotional flyer announces Syracuse University’s second game against UConn. It leans into the school palette—bold orange and deep navy—to signal team pride immediately. A key player is centered as the focal point, while a stadium photo sits behind them with a linear burn overlay to add depth and texture without competing for attention.

Designed on Adobe Photoshop, simple angular elements guide the eye toward the center, with a subtle overlay that keeps the look from feeling flat.
