PieceIT

PieceIT is a UI/UX concept that helps people imagine and assemble their ideal living space. The experience starts with the user’s preferences—style, color palette, and room type—and then guides them through building a room by mixing furniture, finishes, and layouts that fit their taste. The goal is to make interior planning approachable even for non-designers: clear choices, instant visual feedback, and an interface that feels calm and modern.

Designed and prototyped in Figma, the system emphasizes readability, large tap targets, and accessible contrast. Users can browse curated sets, swap items with one tap and compare alternative layouts. Throughout the flow, micro-interactions and straightforward labels keep the focus on exploration rather than learning the tool.
